# Radiological Terrorism: Emergency Management Pocket Guide for Clinicians (page)

Source: CDC Radiation Emergencies. URL: https://www.cdc.gov/radiation-emergencies/hcp/radiological-terrorism-toolkit/pocket-guide.html
Last reviewed: April 24, 2024

## Purpose

This pocket guide is a supplement to the CDC training program "Radiological Terrorism: Just in Time Training for Hospital Clinicians." It is a quick reference for clinicians — physicians, nurses, and other health care professionals — who will provide emergency care in a hospital setting after a radiological terrorism incident.

Full PDF: https://www.cdc.gov/radiation-emergencies/media/pdfs/clinicianpocketguide.pdf
